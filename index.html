Click on any icon!
This website is a sensory archiving project created in 2025 by Kim Byul and Kim Ju eun, students of the Class of 2023 at Kaywon University of Art and Design. It features a total of 50 distinct senses, allowing visitors to click on each icon and directly experience visual and auditory sensations. Over the course of approximately two months, we went through numerous trials to design intuitive interactions and an engaging user experience. Through this website, we aim to propose a new way of recording and sharing sensory experiences.
